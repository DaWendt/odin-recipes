Oats


Description

Ever wondered how to start productively into the day? Well wonder no more!
Indruducing the time-honored powerhouse of breakfast: Oats!
The main kicker here is that we will teach you how to avoid a cement paste and enjoy an actually wonderful breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1/2 cup of almond milk
  • 110g of yoghurt
  • 30g of flavoured chocholate protein powder

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in you favourite bowl
  2. Store in fridge overnight
  3. Enjoy the next morning!
